Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)
✅ Prevention Tips

Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).

Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.

Do not click on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media.

Always verify the source before downloading apps or files.

UPI Safety: Remember that receiving money does NOT require scanning a QR code or entering a PIN.

UPI Safety: Double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before making any payment.

Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions.

Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.

Limit the amount of personal information you share on social media.

Log out from shared or public devices after use.
