Responsible Social Media Use Checklist
✅ Do’s

Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings.

Think before posting and share respectful content.

Verify information before sharing it.

Maintain a positive and professional online presence.

Limit screen time and balance online and offline activities.

❌ Don’ts

Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.

Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.

Do not believe or spread fake news or rumors.

Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying.

Do not accept friend requests from unknown people.
